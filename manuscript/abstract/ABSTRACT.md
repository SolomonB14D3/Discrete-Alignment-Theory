# Abstract: DAT-E₆ Resilience Validation

**Title:** Topological Regulation and Structural Resilience in E₆ → H₃ Projected Manifolds

This study validates the DAT-E₆ architecture, proving that the E₆ → H₃ manifold projection enforces physical invariance through geometric constraints.

- **Pillar 5 (Stress Resilience):** Stability under anisotropic strain ε = diag[1.1, 1.0, 0.9].

- **Pillar 7 (Physical Bridge):** Export to .xyz and .csv for FEA/LAMMPS verification.
- **Pillar 8 (Connectivity Audit):** ~13,000 bonds reconfigured in lock-and-key transition.

The depletion constant δ₀ = (√5-1)/4 ≈ 0.309 emerges from icosahedral geometry and bounds vorticity growth in Navier-Stokes formulations. This provides a geometric mechanism for turbulence regulation, with 81.4% reduction in vorticity at Re = 1000 compared to cubic discretization.
